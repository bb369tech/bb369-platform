Curtain Options

These common paths are intended to help customers quickly understand the general differences between budget curtains, standard home upgrades, and premium custom systems.

Option A — Basic Curtains

Simple fabric curtains or basic blinds. Suitable for rental homes, small rooms, or budget-focused projects.

$80 – $300
Best for quick installation and lower-cost needs.
Most Popular

Option B — Standard Home Upgrade

Better materials, improved light control, cleaner appearance, and more balanced performance for daily use.

$300 – $900
Best balance of style, quality, and price.

Option C — Premium Custom Curtains

Custom sizing, higher-end fabrics, layered curtain systems, or motorized curtain setups for full-room upgrades.

$900 – $3000+
Best for luxury homes, larger spaces, and long-term design upgrades.
